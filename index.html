Manifesto

×

The world has changed. This is an age of intelligent machines, cryptographic public goods, and viral memes of global influence. The creatives of tomorrow will need more than they have now. They need a safe place to risk human futures - to embrace and understand that mastery of risk is the path to our survival. These creatives will need a lab, beyond latent space, and beyond anything we - as only human - can imagine. Only by networking these humans together in concert, can we achieve collectively what our individualized libertarian economies can not.


A future lab - where only the most daring creatives can thrive and collaborate in real time. Can share work and ideas without effort, and can discover anything beyond what we have before. Only a few humans will be emotionally strong enough to cross over. To these, if we are lucky enough to find them, we will give -


The labcoat.
